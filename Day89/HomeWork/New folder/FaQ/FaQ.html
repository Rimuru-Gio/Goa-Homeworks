Who are we? ▼
Estate.Inc is the result of our passion to create better quality, improve service in the world of real estate. Most importantly, we offer transparency. Trust our team, and we will take into account all your criteria, take care of your interests and together we will find a new home
How do I start? ▼
You can start by adding a listing to our site, sending us your questions and sharing your requirements and interests regarding the house, and we will help you find it. After that, we can schedule an online or in-person meeting to talk through the entire process and explain how the market works. If you like our service, we can start working together to help you sell or buy a house.
How To Add My Building Listing ▼
You can add your building listing by going to the dashboard and clicking on 'Add Listing' Or scroll down and see it at the bottom. Fill out the required details and submit.
How To Edit My Listing ▼
To edit your listing, go to 'My Listings', select the listing you want to edit, and update the necessary details.
How To Delete My Listing ▼
Navigate to 'My Listings', choose the listing you want to delete, and click the delete button.

What area does your agency cover? ▼
We will cover all areas of Georgia. We promise you quality and good service at the location of your interest.
What language should the registration documents be in? ▼
Registration documents must be in Georgian and English. Our team of experts also includes translators who can provide consultation.
When does real estate registration take place? ▼
Once the full amount is transferred to the property owner/seller, the documentation is then registered and the buyer is granted official property ownership status.
